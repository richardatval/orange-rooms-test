Welcome

Orange Rooms is a meeting room and conference venue in Leicester.

We have flexible and fully accessible conference and meeting rooms to hire, all finished to a high standard with IT equipment included. Our on-site catering and dedicated staff will be on hand throughout the day to ensure that your event runs smoothly.

If you are looking for a venue in Leicester City Centre, then Orange Rooms is perfect choice for your next event.

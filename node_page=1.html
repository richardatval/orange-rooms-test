Booking Orange Rooms makes a difference

Tuesday 22 January 2013

Booking your meetings, conferences or events at Orange Rooms can make a difference to people lives.

It helps to fund Voluntary Action Leicestershire, who provides advice, guidance and information to the thousands of voluntary organisations throughout Leicester & Leicestershire.

VALUES supports people with learning disabilities and are one of the groups that benefits from proceeds made by Orange Rooms.
